Tutoring Program

About the Program

The Tutoring program was created by the Academic Success Team at UMSI and is dedicated to supporting students inside and outside of the classroom. The UMSI Peer Tutoring Program is an opportunity for UMSI students and students taking UMSI courses to engage with peer tutors and recieve assistance and support in UMSI courses. There are two Peer Tutoring Offerings: Programming Peer Tutoring and Math Peer Tutoring.

Programming Tutoring

The programming Peer Tutoring is available for the following courses:

  • SI 106 Programs, Information, and People
  • SI 206 Data-Oriented Programming
  • SI 506 Programming I
  • SI 507 Intermediate Programming
  • General Python support

For any questions regarding the Programming Peer Tutoring email Porfessor Anthony Whyte at arwhyte@umich.edu

Math Tutoring

UMSI Math Peer Tutoring is available for the below subject areas

  • Linear Algebra
  • Statistics
  • General Math related course support
