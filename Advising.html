Academic Advising Resource Site

There are many resources available for students in the Bachelor of Science in Information (BSI) Program from academic advising, academic planning, and student support resources. This site provides links and condenses these resources into one location.

Academic Advising

The academic advising process allows BSI students to plan, review, and execute their academic plans with guidance from a qualified UMSI Academic Advisor. These academic advisors are there to help you achieve your academic goals and make the most of your resources here at Michigan.

What can your academic advisor help with?

  • Creating an personalized academic plan
  • Interpreting an academic policy or procedure
  • Discussing concerns about a particular course
  • Connecting with student support resources and services
  • Navigating a disruption to you BSI experience

BSI Degree Requirements

Understanding the BSI Degree Requirements is essential for academic success.

General Requirements

  • 120 Credits Total Needed to Graduate with BSI Degree
  • 55-60+ credits before UMSI (including credits brough to UMICH)
  • 45 Upper Level SI Credits
  • 15 Upper Level Credits (level 300+)
